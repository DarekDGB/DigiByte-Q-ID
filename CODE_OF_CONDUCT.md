# CODE OF CONDUCT

## DigiByte Q-ID — v1.0.1

---

## 🤝 Our Commitment

We are committed to creating a respectful, focused, and professional environment.

This project exists to build **secure, deterministic infrastructure** for everyone.

---

## 🌱 Expected Behavior

All contributors are expected to:

- Be respectful and constructive
- Focus on facts, not opinions
- Communicate clearly and honestly
- Support a security-first mindset
- Accept feedback with professionalism

---

## 🚫 Unacceptable Behavior

The following will not be tolerated:

- Harassment or personal attacks
- Disrespectful or aggressive language
- Spreading misinformation
- Ignoring security concerns
- Introducing unsafe or unverifiable changes

---

## 🧭 Contribution Mindset

When contributing:

- Prioritize correctness over speed
- Prefer clarity over cleverness
- Prefer explicit behavior over implicit behavior
- Prefer safety over convenience

---

## ⚖️ Enforcement

Maintainers reserve the right to:

- Reject contributions
- Request changes
- Remove harmful or unsafe content
- Restrict participation if necessary
